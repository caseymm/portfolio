Front End
Work
HOPE
Built with d3, this graphic displays information related to the number of students attending school on a HOPE scholarship for a sampling of schools in Georgia.
Lottery
These choropleth comparison maps are built with d3. One displays where HOPE scholarships were received, while the other diplays where lottery tickets were sold. Clicking on the counties will provide more detailed information and allows for comparison between two counties.
Rain
Compares precipiation to date (as of June 30, 2013) in the 50 most populated cities in the United States. Data is presented through an animated svg that fills on dropdown select.
Six °
This project runs off of the d3 force-based layout and is used here to show the relationship between actors and actresses who have recently filmed projects in Georgia.
Rail
This map displays the 10 most potentially hazardous rail crossings in Georgia. It is built in leaflet.js.
Calendar
Though there is currently "dummy" information in this calendar, I promise it is fully functional. Running off of tabletop.js, it allows for the admin to enter all of the information for the month into a Google spreadsheet. The information (in div form), will then be revealed on the specified day of the month. The calendar pop-up also loads to the current day by default. A separate version running in November-December is based on the day of the year.
Stress
I created this project to measure campus stress level. The project runs off of google forms and spreadsheets and updates when new votes are cast.
