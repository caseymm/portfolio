About
My name is Casey and I’m a rising senior at UNC Chapel Hill majoring in Journalism with a specialization in Multimedia. I’ve put this site together as a way to showcase some of the work that I’ve done.

In my spare time I practice Tae Kwon Do and catch up on tv shows that I've missed during the week. I also enjoy taking pictures, but only as a hobby.

 Please feel free to take a look around and let me know if you have any questions or comments. I look forward to hearing from you.

I created this interactive for the 2013 Peachtree Road Race. The interactive provided relevant information for the race day, including maps and a live twitter feed. The 2013 "Outfits" section was added to the page and updated after the race.
